Introduction

Pick and Pose is an app for posing 3d characters allowing artists to create custom poses and using them as references for their art.

Working with characters

Adding a character

To create a character, click the 'Add Characters' button in the Models panel at the top right. From the dropdown menu, select the desired character to add it to the scene.

Selecting and posing joints

To pose the character, click on one of the blue markers located on the character's joints. Rotating a marker will rotate the corresponding body part.

Switching between characters

If you have multiple characters in the scene, you can switch between them using the Models panel. The selected model is highlighted with a gray background. You can also hide models by clicking on the eye icon next to it.

Character settings

Once you select a character, you can edit its properties. You can adjust the size and color, choose from various body poses, and save custom poses to load later if needed. You can also select hand poses, mirror the pose, duplicate the character, or delete it.

Adding & Manipulating Objects

Adding items (props, hair meshes, accessories)

To add various items to the scene, click on the 'Add Objects' button in the Models panel and click on the desired model.

Using 3D text

To add 3D text, click the 'T' icon in the Models panel. Enter your text, then click the 'Add Text' button to apply it.
