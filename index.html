From Pose to Art in Seconds Build, adjust, and perfect your character references with ease.

Multi-Character Posing Import multiple characters and pose them freely by manipulating joints.

Pose Library & Custom Saves Choose from predefined poses, create your own, and save/load poses for each character.

3D Text Support Type text and convert it into 3D object. Quickly add labels to keep track of characters and details.

Props & Accessories Add tools, chairs, hair, weapons and many more.

Character Customization Adjust model body type and other features.

Attach Hair Quickly move hair mesh to selected character.

Scene Customization Adjust lighting, background color, outlines, shadows, and camera settings.

Save & Export Save full scenes, load on multiple machines, and export as a JPG image when you're done.
