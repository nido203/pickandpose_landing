Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01/01/2025

This Privacy Policy explains how your data is collected, used, and protected when you visit our website or use our application (“the App”).

1. No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII), such as your name, email address, or contact details.

2. License Handling (Non-Persistent, Non-Personal)

When using the App, basic license data is processed to enable software activation and verification. This includes:

  • License Key
  • Instance ID (a non-personal, unique identifier)
  • Date of Activation
  • License validation status

This data is not stored on our servers. It is relayed in real-time to the Lemon Squeezy API for the purposes of:

  • Validating license keys
  • Activating/deactivating licenses
  • Ensuring license compliance

Our backend server acts only as a bridge between the App and Lemon Squeezy. We do not save, or persist any license-related or user data. The data might be logged temporarily for the purpose of debugging.

For more information, please review the Lemon Squeezy Privacy Policy.

3. Website Analytics

We use Google Analytics to measure visits and general usage patterns on our landing page and App. Google Analytics may collect anonymized information such as:

  • Device and browser type
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on the site
  • Referring source

Google Analytics anonymizes IP addresses and does not provide us with any personal information. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

4. Cookies

We use cookies only through Google Analytics to understand general site usage. These cookies do not track individual behavior or serve ads.

5. Data Security

Although we do not store data, we implement secure protocols (e.g., HTTPS, API key validation) to ensure secure communication between your app and our license server.

6. Your Rights

As we do not store any user data or personal information, there is no data for you to access, delete, or correct. If you have concerns or questions, feel free to contact us.

7. Contact

If you have any questions, you can reach us at:

Email: support@pickandpose.com
